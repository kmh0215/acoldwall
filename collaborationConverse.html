ACW* X Converse

ACW_CNVS
Geo Forma for Holiday 2022.

Geo Forma is the latest footwear concept
from ACW_CNVS.
A progressive silhouette built from an elevated
application of material and technology.
Taking shape over a 24 month development
period, the Geo Forma introduces a new luxury
model informed by A-COLD-WALL*’s pursuit
to transform perceptions of product capabilities
through concept and innovative fabrication.
A pioneer in it’s category, the Geo Forma
advances A-COLD-WALL*’s visual language
while establishing a new lens
for Converse footwear.

TALC

At once minimal and monumental, this silhouette-focused iteration of Geo Forma draws its influences from an architectural sensibility. Its refined tooling and distinct, organic angularity is reflective of a design ethos that champions purity of form and uncompromised perspectives.

Lancey Foux Debuts Geo Forma in 032c

British rapper Lancey Foux debuts the Geo Forma in an exclusive interview with 032c.

VOLT

Emboldened by a matured hypergraphic language,
a volt colourway presents a dynamic interpretation
of the Geo Forma. Optimised by A-COLD-WALL*’s
technical fabrication, dual-branded typography
configured into an allover design scheme accentuates
a silhouette that seeks to innovate and disrupt.

ACW* & Converse
- ACW_CNVS Sponge CX Crater

A-COLD-WALL* and Converse unite
once again to deliver a brand new
silhouette, the Sponge CX Crater,
a progressive footwear concept
combining avant-garde design and
comfort-focused technology.
