Autumn/Winter 2022 Collection

The Autumn/Winter 2022 collection is driven by a pursuit of form and gestural expression. Through material exploration, advanced technical processes and graphic-led narratives, the collection seeks to outwardly express the inflections and cadences of an artist’s hand.

Cubist Collection

Layered graphics drive the Cubist collection, where seasonal marble print patches overlaid with block primary colours establish a unique dialogue between key tenets of Classicist and Modernist design. Sitting across a number of refined jersey styles, this graphic development continues to advance A-COLD-WALL*’s visual vocabulary.

Brush Stroke Treatment

Heavily distressed brush strokes become a signature motif for Autumn/Winter 2022. Abstracted and distorted, allover graphic applications provide a tactile and immersive quality that speaks to the collection’s wider design rationale.

Form Gilet

A brand signature, the Form Gilet encompasses A-COLD-WALL*’s relationship with material and form. Equipped with inverted embellishments in the form of interior pockets and hidden snap buttons, the garment explores prospects of functionality and concealment. For Autumn/Winter 2022, the Form Gilet is treated with the season’s blurred print in addition to the classic black iteration.

Studio Moc

The Studio Moc is a refined development within the category, featuring a nylon sock with an adjustable drawcord to optimise comfort and fit. A grounding Vibram flex rubber sole provides a structural support to this season’s loafer, retaining functionality and utility as key design considerations. vocabulary.
