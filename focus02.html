SS23 Industrial
Compositions

Spring/Summer 2023 "Industrial Compositions"
Paris Fashion Week Installation

Concept

A site-specific, audiovisual exhibition designed in-house by A-COLD-WALL*’s industrial design team to provide immersive access to the brand universe. Amplified by an original soundscape performed by Kelvin Krash, the two-day presentation connected attributes of static presentation, live performance and installation, debuting a hybrid format that reevaluated traditional modes of display.

Engineer. Shape. Synchronicity.

280mm x 140mm. 28 pages. Adiron Blue 270gsm foiled cover. Mohawk Superfine White. 148gsm inner.

An interplay between hand built treatments
and technical fabrications.

Dyes, washes, paints and wireframes provided a conduit to merge technical poise with artisanal
sensibilities. Sophisticated garment construction undergoing rounds of hand-applied treatments
showcased the expressive presence of the artist’s hand and the addition of an atelier prospect
to the brand’s output.
