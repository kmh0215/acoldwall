ACW* X Dr.Martens

A-COLD-WALL* & Dr. Martens for Autumn/Winter 2022

Obstruct. Function. Innovate.
The latest volume of this partnership expresses a modernist interpretation of signature Dr. Martens styles, whilst studying the relationship between light and shade. Steeped in tradition while boldly facing the future, comes another synthesis of A-COLD-WALL*’s design narrative with the progressive ethos of Dr. Martens.

1461 Gehry Shoe in Onyx

Exclusive to a-cold-wall.com
An ACW* exclusive, the Onyx colourway is an all black iteration accented only by Dr. Marten’s trademark welt yellow stitching. Stacked on a double-height Bex sole are subtle architectural details that speak to a A-COLD-WALL*’s design language. Gunmetal rivets, embossed leather and sculptural side panels contribute a contemporary visual vocabulary to this storied silhouette.

1460 Gehry Boot in Bone

The 1460 boot is reinterpreted in a stark,
monochromatic colourway. The allover white palette extends to a white zipper and transparent sole, bold visual additions that optimises the boots’ postmodern aesthetic.

The campaign shot in deep East-London by Jesse Crankson, stars long-term brand friend, poet and thought-leader Wilson Oyrema. Styling from Karlmond Tang from current season and the brand archive, embodies notions of sophistication and modernity - paralleling the spirit of the partnership between both Dr. Martens and Wilson Oryema.

1461 Gehry Shoe in Bone

In keeping with the progressive notes of this collection, the 1461 shoe in bone synchronises futurist design elements to drive forward the attitudinal essence of this signature silhouette. Deeply embossed premium leather, exaggerated side panels and a front zip further articulate an evolved interpretation of this classic model.
