  • SHOPPING & RETURN

    Online Shopping

    • Do you process exchanges? - We do not offer exchanges at this time. Please return
      your unwanted order through our Return Centre and then purchase
      your preferred item in a new order.
    • Can I cancel my order? - Orders are processed automatically,
      therefore, once you receive confirmation of your order unfortunately
      it cannot be cancelled. If you wish to return your order,
      please first check if it is eligible (see ‘Which items are returnable’ below.)
      If it meets the conditions of return, proceed to the Return Centre.
    • What does my Order Status mean? - Processing - Your payment has been received
      Completed - The order has been fulfilled and completed
      On-hold - Your item has been reserved but we are awaiting your payment
      Cancelled - The order has been cancelled.
      Refunded - The order has been fully refunded.
      Partially refunded - Certain items within your order have been refunded.
      This often refers to a sale where either products are cancelled
      but there are remaining items in the order to be fulfilled or when an
      order is only partially returned.
    • Which Payment Methods can I use? - We accept the following payment methods:
      Visa, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, Visa Electron,
      Maestro enabled debit cards.
      A-COLD-WALL* offers a fast and convenient purchase method
      allowing you to save your credit card and shipping details for
      future purchases.
      We also accept a selection of fast & convenient payment methods
      allowing you to purchase without entering your credit card details
      on the website. Your account will be charged once the order is completed.
      These include: Paypal, Apple Pay, Amazon Pay and Google Pay.

    International Return

    • I purchased a collaborative item, can I return it? - All sales are final for collaborative items,
      therefore we cannot accept return of these items.
    • Which items are returnable? - Faulty and unused non-collaborative items may be returned to A-COLD-WALL*.
      Please ensure before you return an item that all tags are
      attached and all original packaging is present and intact.
      For footwear, please place the shoebox inside protective packaging.
      You have 14 days from the date of delivery to return
      items purchased on A-COLD-WALL*.
    • How do I request a return? - If your item is eligible for return under the conditions stated above,
      please visit the Return Centre and follow the instructions below:
      Insert your order number and email address
      Select the item you want to return
      State the reason for your return
      Select your preferred refund method, either the original payment method
      or store credit
      Select your preferred shipping method if prompted
      Your return will then be processed by customer service who will share
      further instructions via email.
      Please note that responsibility and cost for the safe return of the item
      rests with the customer. We strongly advise you to return the item using
      a tracked service. Original shipping costs will not be refunded and inbound
      costs will not be covered. A-COLD-WALL* cannot expedite or track
      returned packages.
    • Do you offer exchanges? - We do not offer exchanges at this time. Please return your
      unwanted order through our Return Centre and then purchase
      your preferred item in a new order.
    • When will my refund be issued? - Once we receive your returned item we will issue a refund
      via your original payment method, please allow 5-7 working days
      for this to be processed.
      Please note - duties and customs paid at delivery are not refundable.
      To enquire if you are eligible for a duty/customs refund,
      please contact your local customs agency directly.
      If you have any questions regarding this process or need
      assistance please contact our support team.
    • What do I do if my item is faulty? - If your item is faulty, please visit our Return Centre,
      enter your Order Number, state your reason for return as FAULTY ITEM,
      describe the fault and additionally, provide pictures of the fault.
      Our team will then process your return request.
    • What if my A-COLD-WALL* item is faulty but I did not purchase it from the A-COLD-WALL* website? - If your item was not purchased from the A-COLD-WALL* website
      we unfortunately cannot accept it as a return. Please refer to the
      returns policy of the retailer from which you originally purchased the item
      or contact them directly to discuss further.
