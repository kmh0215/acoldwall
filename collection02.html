Pre-Fall 2022

Built to adapt to a range of environments and terrains, Pre-Fall 2022 proposes a series of intricate layers offered in varying properties and weights. The interplay of light and dark defines this collection’s palette. Greys and blacks form a grounding base, a stable backdrop to saturated shades of volt blue, volt yellow and rich orange - infusing everyday jersey, complex knits and functional accessories with a tangible energy.

M-65

Furthering study into the signature M65 jacket, the classic silhouette is updated with minimalist design sensibilities for Pre-Fall 2022. Lightweight and versatile, this season’s model is constructed from durable 3-layer plain weave fabric paired with performance membrane, while customisable features reiterate a value system built on adaptability and engagement.

Volt Blue

Interspersed across the Pre-Fall 2022 collection, Volt Blue is established as a key signifier within A-COLD-WALL*’s visual vocabulary. This volt colour scheme serves as a device to connect brand activity across ready-to-wear, printed material and retail spaces.

Green Ochra

For Pre-Fall 2022, primary colours are skewed and adjusted to hues connected to the Autumnal landscape, providing a palette that is at once seasonal while signalling to the brand’s broader colour program.

Brutalist graphic

Referent to the brand’s interest in architecture, the Brutalist graphic print is revisited for Pre-Fall 2022. Redeveloped through a three-dimensional textured puff print, the signature graphic receives a tactile finish across all key jersey silhouettes.
