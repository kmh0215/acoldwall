Service Point 1

Service Point 1 [SP1]

Service Point 1 reflects a value system built upon channels of community interaction, offering select individuals the opportunity to engage with product, concept and process through garment and installation. The M-65, a timeless menswear staple loaded with meaning and history, provided a vehicle to exhibit and explore A-COLD-WALL*’s focus on fabrication, material property, uniformity & reductive function.

Material Focus

Dyes, washes, paints and wireframes provided a conduit to merge technical poise with artisanal sensibilities. Sophisticated garment construction undergoing rounds of hand-applied treatments showcased the expressive presence of the artist’s hand and the addition of an atelier prospect to the brand’s output.

Output

Sharing process, information, context and product collectively with a global fold of tight knit individuals. Channelling the value systems of open-source behaviour formed the rationale of Service Point 1. 75 M65 jackets were gifted to select participants at no cost, trading time and conversation as a commodity.

Open Sourced Hardware

Open-source as a philosophy brings forth conscious
brand values that have the capacity to directly enhance
interaction between individual and brand.
Hardware packages, inclusive of buckles,
diamond badges and zip pullers enabled individuals
the opportunity to access brand intellectual property.
